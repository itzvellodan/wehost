A team of enthusiastic designers, architects and engineers committed to quality, ceaseless innovation and sustainable vision.

Metarch is a novel venture establishing at kerala to engrave a new era in the metal furnishing industry. Our aim is to offer premium quality products and carefully crafted customised services for our customers thereby enriching their lives in the safest and most responsible way. We are a team of enthusiastic designers,architects and engineers committed to delivering quality products,ceaseless innovation and sustainable vision. We design metal framed furnitures which are cost effective,lightweight and

detachable. These contemporary products not only enhances one’s interior but will also be a boon to flood prone areas. Metarch responds to the ever changing needs of customers by working towards a more sustainable solution and will play a major role in reducing deforestation. Our customers safety and satisfaction are our primary priority and ultimate concern. We make sure to directly import our raw materials from manufacturers and is dealing exclusively with top leading steel brand TATA & APPOLO which is a trusted and premium quality certified steel company in the market.
